Function Centre

The Flagstaff Hill Community Centre boasts one of the best setups in the local community for all sorts of functions big or small. Inside there are multiple rooms, licensed bar, projecter screen and TV (featuring Foxtel and PC input), two kitchens and enough tables and chairs to seat over 100.

Surrounding the centre you will find an oval (Cricket & Football), Cricket Nets, Netball Courts and Tennis Courts, all of which are under lights. Other features of the centre include plenty of room under shelter, a playground, outside speakers for PA System and plenty of room to let the kids run around!

The centre is available for hire either as a whole or individual rooms/areas, for information about this please contact us.
