Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What days and times are trainings held?

A. The Senior Teams (Under 18's, C Grade, B Grade and A Grade) train on Tuesday and Thursday nights at the Flagstaff Hill F.C at 6:00pm for a 6:30pm start. Junior Trainings (Under 14's and Under 16's) are held on Monday and Wednesday nights, starting at 5:45pm for a 6:00pm start. Sub-Junior Teams (Under 8's, Under 10's and Under 12's) train on Friday nights from 6:00pm. These days and times are subject to change, so for more information about specific trainings please contact the respective team coach.

 

Q. What days and times are games played?

A. Unless otherwise stated in the Southern Football Leagues fixtures the A Grade starts at 2:30pm, B Grade at 12:50pm, Under 18's at 11:10am, Under 16's at 9:35am and the Under 14's at 8:15am on Saturdays. The C Grade starts at 1:00pm on Sundays, while the Sunday Under 14's start at 12:20pm and the Sunday Under 16's start at 1:40pm. The Under 8's kick off at 9:00am, Under 10's at 10:00am and the Under 12's at 11:00am.

 

Q. I'm new to the area, who should I contact about joining the Flagstaff Hill Football Club?

A. The best way to contact the club for upcoming Training Times or to organise to speak to a Coach, is via our Club Email Address: fhfc@internode.on.net. As a new member to the club, you will need to fill in a club Registration Form, and possibly a Southern Football League Registration Form before you are eligible to play.

 

Q. I currently play for another team, but want to move to the Flagstaff Hill Football Club, what do I need to do?

A. Simply come out to Training and speak to your Team Coach who will be able to supply you with the correct paperwork to finalise a clearance from a different club and/or league.

 

 

If you have any questions which are not available on this page, then please feel free to email us at fhfc@internode.on.net
