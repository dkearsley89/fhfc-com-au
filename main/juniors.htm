Juniors

Welcome to the Flagstaff Hill Football Clubs Junior area for Season 2017, on this page you will find all the information associated with the Junior Falcon teams.The SFL has introduced Age Group changes for 2017 & if you have any questions that are not covered on this page or would like something added then please email us, also join the relevant Facebook Group & Team App for up to date news.

Flagstaff Hill currently offer three Saturday sides (Under 13s, Under 15s & Under 17.5's) and two Sunday sides (Under 14s & Under 16s), this provides a great opportunity for all standards of players as well as catering for College students who are required to play for their school on a Saturday.

Success has been a constant for the juniors at Flagstaff Hill, delivering a magnificent 11 Premierships and many more Grand Final appearances over the last 14 seasons, including an Undefeated Under 14's Premiership in 2003 and 2016 plus going back-to-back in the Under 14s in 2003 and 2004, Under 16s in 2010 and 2011, Under 18s in 2012 and 2013 & most recently the Under 14s again in 2015 and 2016.

Under 13s - Saturday (New Age Group for 2017)
The Under 13s are the future A Grade stars for the Falcons and for most players the move from Subbies or School Football to competitive Club Football is a very smooth process, thanks mostly to our hard working coaches and directors. This group has been very successful winning Premierships in 2000, 2003, 2004, 2008, 2015 and 2016 (as U14s)

Craig Bunworth will be in charge of the Saturday Under 13s in 2017. Trainings are held on Monday and Wednesday nights at 6:00pm, players are required to bring a Mouthguard to every training and if they are going to be late or unable to make it, then please contact one of the coaches as early as possible.

Although game times can differ (depending on other grades fixtures) the standard time for kick off for the Saturday team is 8:15am, and for the Sunday team games start at 12:20pm. So if your Son was born in late 2003 or 2004 and would like to play for the Falcons then please get in contact with Craig Bunworth or the Donny Aldridge the FHFC Junior Director.

Under 15s - Saturday (New Age Group for 2017)
The Under 15s have enjoyed continuous success in the Southern Football League, taking home the silverware in Season 2007, 2010 and 2011. Paul Hollands will take charge of the Saturday Under 15s as they look to bounce back from a 5th place result in 2016. Paul is very excited about the talent available this year and is looking forward to coaching the boys to a Flag in 2017.

Training's are held on Monday and Wednesday nights at 6:00pm, players are required to bring a Mouthguard to every training and if they are going to be late or unable to make it, then please contact one of the coaches as early as possible.

Although game times can differ (depending on other grades fixtures) the standard time for kick off for the Saturday team is 9:35am, and for the Sunday team games start at 1:40pm. So if your Son was born in late 2001 or 2002 then please get in contact with either Paul or Donny Aldridge the FHFC Junior Director.

Sat U15s Coach - Paul Hollands - Mob: 0404 022 488 Email: paulthea@internode.on.net

Under 17.5s - Saturday (New Age Group for 2017)
Although the Under 17.5s train with the Senior squad they are still regarded as Juniors by the SFL, for Season 2017, past player & Premiership winning Coach Craig Burfitt will again be in control of the team. This will be Craig's 8th year in charge of the Under 17.5s (formerly U18s) and his 11th year of coaching juniors here at FHFC. This age Group has also been successful winning Premierships in 2005, 2012 and 2013.

Trainings are held on Monday and Thursday nights at 6:00pm for a 6:30pm start, if players are going to be late or unable to make it, then please contact Craig (see below). Although game times can differ (depending on other grades fixtures) the standard time for kick off for the Under 17.5s is 11:10am.

So if your Son was born in late 1999 or 2000 then please get in contact with Craig Burfitt or Donny Aldridge the FHFC Junior Director.

New Players
The Flagstaff Hill Football Club welcomes all new players to come out and play for the Falcons, if you are interested in playing or have a friend that may want to join then please contact one of the above coaches or email us.

Junior Fees
It costs well over $150,000 to run our football club each year plus thousands of hours of work provided free of charge by volunteers. Paying your membership fees on time is a small way for you to show respect to your Club, to your team mates and to those volunteers whose efforts allow you to play football each week.

At the Flagstaff Hill Football Club we have a 'No Pay, No Play' policy, this is "if any player has not paid their subs in full or entered into a payment plan with the treasurer by the 6th April 2017, then that player will be ineligible to play until the fees are paid in full".
