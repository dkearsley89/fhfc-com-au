FHFC Good Ideas Register

At the Flagstaff Hill Football Club, we strive to be the best football club in the Southern Football League and beyond – both on and off the field. To do this, we need your ideas and feedback about what we can do to make OUR club even better. We also need even more volunteers to make these great ideas happen.

In 2008, we launched the 'Falcon Good Ideas Register'. We are proud to say that more than 30 of these ideas were implemented in the first year alone.

We're launching the register again this year – so if you have a good idea, or if you would like to become a valued volunteer please email: fhfc@internode.on.net
