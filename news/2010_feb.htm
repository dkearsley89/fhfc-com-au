FHFC Pre-Season Golf Day

Come along to the Football Club's first official social event of Season 2010. From 10:30am on Sunday the 14th of March at the immaculate Echunga Golf Course. Entry will be $30 per player and golfers will be teamed up in groups of 4, playing a version of golf known as 'Ambrose'.

Your $30 entry fee gets you 18 holes of gold, a free BBQ after your round, your first drink of the day free and a chance to win many great prizes. Also on the day we will be holding a longest drive competition and several putting challenges.

Numbers for the day are limited so please contact David Kearsley on 0424 384 312 or email dkearsley@internode.on.net to book your spot. Please note that clubs are not available to hire, but due to the nature of the event (playing in teams) sharing golf clubs is perfectly fine.

For more information please see this flyer.

2010 Falcon Family Fun Day / Registration Day

Come along on Sunday the 21st of February to relax and enjoy our Falcon Family Fun Day and Registration Day. Everyone from Auskick to A Grade is invited to attend, as well as family, partners, friends and sponsors.

All players are asked to make it easier on the club and register and pay your fees on the day. Aside from the registration facilities there will be fun footy competitions, face painting, giveaways, merchandise, performance mouthguards on site, food & music, plus much more!!!

Please BYO chairs and shades if sunny. BBQ and canteen available but feel free to BYO food / nibbles to share with friends.

We look forward to seeing you on the day. For more information phone Paul McGuire on mobile 0419 824 089 or view the event flyer.

 

Loxton North Trial Game & Club Day

The Flagstaff Hill Under 18's, B Grade and A Grade will be playing the clubs first trial game against Loxton North Football Club in the Riverland on Saturday the 20th of March 2010.

The trial game is also a great opportunity for members, supporters and players at the Club to enjoy a social weekend away together on the banks of the Murray. Following the trial games on Saturday we'll enjoy a meal at the Loxton North Football Club followed by a social night around a camp fire.

After a bacon and egg brekky on Sunday morning you can spend the day waterskiing, kneeboarding, wakeboarding, playing cricket or whatever else takes your fancy.

We want as many people to come as possible. This will be a great opportunity to show support for our senior teams, have some fun, get to know someone new (or old) AND kick off Soar 2010 as a strong, committed and unified Club. More details will follow soon. Importantly – put it in your calendar and get your site or cabin booked. Please also let Jo or Darren Collins know if you’re planning to come for the weekend on 0408 000 650 or email jo.collins@sa.gov.au or darrenc@accesshardware.com.au

At the FHFC we strive to be the best football club in the Southern Football League and beyond - both on and off the field. To do this, we need your ideas and feedback about what we can do to make our club even better.

In 2008 we launched the 'Falcon Good Ideas Register' and we are proud to say that we have acted on more than 30 of these ideas already. We're launching the register again in 2010, so if you have a good idea, or if you would like to become a valued volunteer please email club President Brett Charlesworth or use our feedback form.

As most of the club would be aware, training has commenced for all grades except the Sub-Juniors. Our goal for all Junior and Senior players and teams is to be the best we can be, and you can't achieve this without a 100% commitment to training. The club has worked hard to provide every player with exceptional facilities, coaches and programs to help you improve your football. Training days and times are as follows:

Senior's and Under 18's Tuesday & Thursday - 6:00pm for a 6:30pm start
Under 16's Monday & Wednesday - 5:50pm for a 6:00pm start
Under 14's Tuesday & Thursday - 4:50pm for a 5:00pm start
Sub Juniors To Be Advised
All times are correct unless otherwise notified by your coach.
