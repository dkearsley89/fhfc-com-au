2015 Player Registration Day

All players need to register. Our Registration Day and Family Fun Day is happening in Sunday 8th February. Full details can be seen on this flyer. Feel free to share this attachment around and actively encourage as many people and players (sub junior, junior and senior) as you can to join us at Flagstaff Hill in 2015.

 

2015 FHFC Senior & Under 18s Season Launch

Although we are not commencing pre-season training until after Christmas we will be holding a season launch meeting on Tuesday, November the 18th at 7:30pm at FHFC. Rod Mitchell (A Grade Coach) would love to see all players from 2014 and encourage everyone to bring along a mate who could potentially play in the red and blue in 2015.

The B Grade and Under 18 coaches will be announced on the night as well as new signings plus Jarryd Wallace will present players with pre-season training programs.

Player-Coach meetings will commence after November the 18th with both the A and B Grade Coaches (An online calendar will be available to book in your meeting timeslot).

Don't forget that 2015 is 'Back to Flaggy' year and we will be working hard to get our past players either back on the track or at least coming back to the club to support the Falcons.
