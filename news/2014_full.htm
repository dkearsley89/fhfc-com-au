FHFC Annual Bingo Night

On Saturday the 5th of July the Flagstaff Hill F.C will be holding its annual Bingo Night. Please see this flyer for full details.

This is a FAMILY FRIENDLY event that suits Subbies through to Seniors and we would love to see as many people as possible attend. Nat Bunworth will be hosting the evening meal and running her Sweets ‘n’ Treats for a Cure from 6.00pm. All moneys raised will go towards beating Neuroblastoma.

We are encouraging all families to attend the dinner, stay for club presentations at 7.00pm and then enjoy a night of fun and frivolity at Bingo. This is a great opportunity for Sub-Junior families to be involved with the Junior and Senior teams and we especially encourage Under 12 players and their families who will move to Junior football in 2015.

 

FHFC Masquerade Ball

One of the biggest nights on the FHFC Social Calendar is fast approaching... The FHFC Masquerade Ball will be held on Saturday the 24th of May from 8:00pm (following the game against Port Noarlunga).

The event is being held in the Bignell Room at the Bay Function Centre. Dress is Cocktail including your all important mask!

Tickets are $50 per head and this includes finger food and subsidised drinks. Prizes will be awarded throughout the night, so make sure you get along to the 2014 ball!

2014 Player Registration and Family Fun Day

Update: The Member Fees for 2014 have now been finalised, please see this flyer for more info!

The 2014 Season is fast approaching and so is the 2014 Player Registration and Family Fun Day. The day will be on Sunday the 9th of February from 3pm to 7pm. We ask that all existing and new players (from all grades) come along to fill in the required paperwork and pay their membership fees for 2014

This year there will be plenty of entertainment, including:

  • Bull Riding Competition (With prizes!)
  • Jumpy Castle
  • Dunking Machine
  • Face Painters
  • Charley the Peanut!
  • Performance Mouthguards - For professional mouthguard fitting (3pm-6pm)
  • Wine Tasting
  • The Canteen, BBQ and Bar will be open all day!
  • Trainers display, Footy Boot sale, Falconettes Lounge and much more!

More information on Fees will be released shortly, in the meantime lock the date away in your diary. We look forward to seeing all of our players on the day! (especially our Senior Players...)

 

2014 Senior & Under 18's Pre-Season Training

Pre-Season commences on Monday the 13th of January 2014 for all Senior (A, B and C Grade) and Under 18 players at 6:15pm for a 6:30pm start. Training will take place at Flinders Uni on the bottom oval, closest to South Road. Players are required to bring Football Boots and Runners.

Training will be held by new fitness coach Jarryd Wallace and the Senior Coaching Staff. We encourage any new players to come along... Training will continue to be held at Flinders Uni on Monday and Thursday nights at 6:30pm until further notice.

Pre-Season Launch - Seniors & Under 18's

Just a reminder that next Tuesday night, 12th November at 7.30 pm at the Footy Club we are holding our Pre-Season Launch. This is to communicate all developments so far for 2014, introduce you to our Fitness Coordinator and set some programs in place for everyone.

As you know there is no official training until after Christmas. All these details will be explained on the night. If you’re an Under 18 or Senior Player and are planning on playing in Season 2014 (and winning premierships)... then you should be there. Even if you are only half thinking about it you should be there to get all the information you’ll need to make your decision.

Please share this information around with all your footy mates so we get a big turn up on the night and set the right mood for next year right from the start. Coaches, Club Officials and all other interested Supporters are more than welcome to attend.

 

FHFC AGM

The Annual General Meeting of the Flagstaff Hill Football Club will be held on Tuesday the 8th October at 7.30pm in the Clubrooms. The main purpose of the meeting is to receive the Annual Report and to elect our new Committee.

Here's your chance to make a difference... We need some more NEW people on the Committee and on our Sub Committees. We need more Senior & Under 18 Players & Partners and Junior & Sub Junior Parents... The Committee only meets 5 or 6 times per year and the Sub Committees only meet as required.

If you want to make a positive impact on our Footy Club and help it to improve then don't just talk about it... Get onto the committee so you can make a difference!
