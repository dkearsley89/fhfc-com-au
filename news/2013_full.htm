Pre-Season News Update

Season 2013 is just around the corner, please see below for some key upcoming dates:

Footy Camp: There’ll be a dedicated Footy Camp at Morgan for the Seniors and Under 18’s on the weekend of Feb 23rd. This is a professionally run camp focusing on Leadership and Team Building and will be alcohol free. This will be like nothing we have ever offered before at Flagstaff Hill and could make a huge difference to our results this year... but it will only do so if we get maximum attendance. Lock the date away NOW. More details very soon.

Under 14's & 16's Pre-Season Training: Training starts for 14’s and 16’s on Mon 4th Feb. Meet at the Seacliff Yacht Club carpark for a 6pm start. Bring running gear for the first night. All other details will be provided on the night.

Family Fun Day: The Family Fun Day is scheduled for Sun, 10th Feb from 12.00 to 4.00. We STRONGLY encourage all Seniors, Juniors and Sub Juniors to register on this day. Stay for a few hours and join in the fun and activities or just come for 10 minutes to get the paperwork and fees sorted out. Heaps more details coming soon.

 

2013 Return of Pre-Season Training - Seniors & Under 18's

Training for the Seniors and Under 18's will recommence on Tuesday the 29th of January. Please meet at the Football Club at 6:15pm for a 6:30pm start. Thursday (Jan 31st) will be at Flinders Uni (Lower Ground - Closest to South Road), 6:15pm for a 6:30pm start.

Also please book these dates in your diary for our Trial Games:
Friday March 8th vs Blackwood @ Blackwood (A's & B's) - Under Lights
Saturday March 16th vs Onkaparinga Valley @ FHFC
Saturday March 23rd vs Loxton North @ Loxton North - You may want to book accommodation ASAP.

 

2013 Pre-Season Training - Seniors & Under 18's

We will be kicking off Season 2013 with a Pre-Season Launch on the 27th of November at the club from 6:15pm, the meeting will take approximately 1 hour followed by a light run for the players. Everyone is encouraged to come, even if you are not sure about playing in 2013 - this is for ALL Senior and Under 18 players.

You can help the club by spreading the word to make sure we get as many people to the meeting as possible. Tell your mates, text them, email them, tweet them, Facebook them, do what ever you need to do to get them there!
