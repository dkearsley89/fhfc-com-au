Round 2 Wrap Up

After the first full round of the SFL's 2011 Season the Falcons are sitting pretty in all grades, albeit after playing one of the cellar dwellers for this year. The Under 18's enjoyed another week off with the bye, while all other grades played Marion at Home on Saturday.

Craig Bahnisch grabbed his first win as Under 14's coach with a comfortable victory against the Rams in the pouring rain. Liam O'Brien led the goal kicking charts with 3, while several other new players to the club made an instant impression at the Falcons.

The Under 16's carried on from last season's premiership with a crushing 33 goal victory. Shane Heatley showed that he will be an important player for the Falcons this year with 4 goals and plenty more touches.

The B Grade started a bit slow, but under new coach Rod Mitchell they quickly picked up the pace. Jarrad Lee pushed his case for A Grade selection with a few goals in a Best On Ground performance.

John Ramage and the A Grade Squad got off to a good start in Season 2011, with a 20 goal win over Marion. Sam Smith took multiple hangers and slotted through 10 goals in a very entertaining display from the goal square. While down back Steven Gater patrolled his troops and provided plenty of run out of defence.

Wal Davison

Wal passed away peacefully at Flinders Private Hospital on Friday March 4th, aged 84. Wal was president of our Club when it first became incorporated as the Flagstaff Hill Football Club in 1979 and remained so for 4 years until 1982.

The Davison family have a long and successful association with our Club, Wal was a foundation member and president, Greg is a life member and played over 200 senior games while grandsons Mark, Joel and Luke all played senior footy. The Club would like to pass on its best wishes to the Davison family. Without the history created by men like Wal our Club wouldnâ€™t have a successful present or fantastic future. RIP Wal.

2011 FHFC Falcon Family Fun Day

One of the most important days for the Falcon Community is coming up on Sunday the 13th of February. Starting from 11:00am and concluding in the late afternoon the Flagstaff Hill Football Club will be holding their annual Falcon Family Fun Day & Registration Day at the Flagstaff Hill Oval and Clubrooms.

A major part the day involves registration for all players, right from Subbies to A Graders. All new players are welcome to register on the day, but please make sure to bring your birth certificate or proof of age.

There will be plenty of fun activites on offer for all ages including:

  • 3 Wheel Trike Rides (for all ages)
  • Performance Mouthguards onsite to fit Junior & Senior's
  • Scooby Doo Jumpy Castle
  • Charley Peanut (with free giveaways)
  • Club Merchandise (including Falconette sign ups)
  • Face Painting

We would really like to see the entire oval and clubrooms packed out! Everyone from Auskick through to the Seniors are asked to attend and bring along Family, Friends and anyone else that is interested in being apart of the Flagstaff Hill Community.
