News Archive - June to September 2010

Pre Season Training This Week

A reminder to all players that training will take place at Seacliff Beach on Tuesday the 21st of December, please arrive at 6:15pm for a 6:30pm start. Training will return to Flagstaff Hill Oval for Thursday. Training will recommence on Tuesday the 18th of January 2011.

 

2011 Pre Season Training - Seniors

Update: Pre Season Training for the Seniors will now commence on the 14th of December at 6:30pm at the Football Club. Please be there no later than 6:15pm, Full Training Schedules and Training Tops will be issued on the night.

Pre Season Training for the entire Senior Squad (Under 18's, C's, B's and A Grade) will commence on the 14th of December at the Flagstaff Hill F.C. More details will be released shortly but we welcome all new players and expect all current players to be present at training. Full training schedules will be released when they have been finalised. Training Tops will once again be handed out this Pre Season at a cost of $5 per player. Look forward to seeing you there!

For more information about Pre-Season or if you have any questions please email us at fhfc@internode.on.net

 

2011 FHFC Season Preview

All players and officials from the A Grade down to the Under 18's are asked to come along to the 2011 Season Preview at the Clubrooms on Thursday the 4th of November from 7:30pm till 8:30pm.

The meeting will allow everyone to meet the coaches, discuss Pre-Season and Recruiting and organise player interviews. We look forward to seeing you there.

 

2010 SFL Under 16's Premiers!

Flagstaff Hill have continued their strong success in the SFL Junior Competition with a 43 point victory over Cove at Hickinbotham Oval. The Falcons never even gave the Cobras a sniff with a 5 goal to zero first quarter all but securing the result.

Huw Channing, Harry Gebhardt (4 Goals), Matt Macbeth and Chris Perrotta (1 Goal) all played great games while Jake Ashworth also contributed with 3 goals of his own.

 

2010 SFL Grand Final

After two fantastic Preliminary Final matches the Flagstaff Hill Football Club has two teams taking part in Grand Finals this weekend. For the 8th consecutive year the FHFC will be represented on Grand Final day and we will hopefully come home Premiership number 7 and 8 from those past 8 seasons.

Under 16's v Cove @ 9:20am

Under 14's v Happy Valley @ 8:00am

Both games will be played at Hickinbotham Oval (South Adelaide's Home Ground), please make sure you get along to both games and support both teams as we try to bring home two premierships this season. Wear your Red and Blue and cheer as loud as you can, the famous Red Army will be congregating on the outer wing. See you at the game!
